About Us

National Steel Trades was founded in year 1965 by Late Shri Jalaluddin Abdul Samad Chaudhary and our business is a professionally managed oragnization providing best quality Iron & steel to various corporate Customers within India.

National Steel Trades began with a clear vision — to build a business that stands on the pillars of quality, integrity, and commitment. From modest beginnings, we have grown into a recognized name in India’s iron and steel industry, known for our unwavering focus on serving our clients with distinction.

Who We Are

We take pride in introducing ourselves as on of the fast growing and the most trusted business partner of some very affluent corporate companies where we have been serving with the best quality material at the most competitive price and giving on time delivery.

We are known for our experience in Iron & Steel trading and client satisfaction. Since our inception, we have worked with our customers in close association & rendered our best possible co-operation and support to our customers in turn, resulted in we earning goood reputation and status with most of our customers.
